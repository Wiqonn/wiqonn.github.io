Research Experience

Wiqonn has been featured in peer-reviewed research journals, demonstrating significant contributions to the field.

1. Influence of preprocessing and segmentation on the complexity of the learning machines in medical imaging.

Journal: International Journal of Artificial Intelligence 2021.
Description: This paper explores the challenges of machine learning in medicine, including balancing human-like cognition with enhanced capabilities, addressing technical issues like artifact removal and image preprocessing, and meeting regulatory standards while developing robust AI decision-making systems for healthcare deployment, ultimately contributing to the advancement of AI-based medical interpretive systems and decision-making frameworks.
Link: PDF.

2. Medical Decision Support System using Weakly-Labeled Lung CT Scans

Journal: Frontiers in Medical Technology 2022.
Description: This paper introduces an AI-based system that helps doctors analyze axial lung CT scans for COVID-19. Using a DenseNet neural network, it classifies scans as normal, COVID-19, or other diseases. Tested on over 1,300 CT studies, the system shows high accuracy and includes features for segmenting and tracking lung lesions, making it a valuable tool for improving clinical decision-making in COVID-19 care.
Link: PDF.
